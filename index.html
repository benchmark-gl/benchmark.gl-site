Benchmark

Benchmark.GL stress tests GPUs and stores the results. The more people who hit the benchmark, the more accurate the data will be. Contribute to the project by benchmarking your device!

Open Data

We then take that data, normalize it, and dump it into an NPM package. You use it to calibrate your WebGL apps to match the capabilities of your users' devices.
