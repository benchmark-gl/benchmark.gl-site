WTF is this

Benchmark.GL is a WebGL benchmark & calibration library. The more people who use the benchmark, the more accurately client performance settings can be calibrated

The Calibrator

The calibration library is an npm installable list of WebGL renderers you can use to pinpoint the performance capabilities of someone using your app. You can weight the performance data so it's even more accurate for your particular use case.

The Benchmark

The benchmark tests texture IO, shaders, and computation. We average and weight them together to get a wholistic view of the performance of a particular GL renderer.
